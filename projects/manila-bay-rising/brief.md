# manila-bay-rising — Story Brief

Created 2026-06-12. Source: user premise (Manila / Manila Bay FMG growth story).
Companion to the Natal/Brazil pairing — fair tourist + dark local, same chemistry.

## Logline
A Korean tourist and a morena Manileña swim in a forbidden, contaminated stretch of
Manila Bay and discover that every spike of emotion — fear, thrill, adrenaline — makes
them grow. They start hunting highs across Metro Manila.

## Setting
Metro Manila. Makati is the heart — skyline, Greenbelt, Poblacion nightlife, casino glow
out at Entertainment City, EDSA noise, money and heat. Manila Bay for the famous sunset
and the new white-sand (dolomite) beach. A cordoned-off stretch — *bawal lumangoy*, no
swimming — because a city outfall dumps runoff straight into the sea. That outfall is the
formula's delivery route.

## Cast
- **Seo Hae-won** — Korean tourist. Fair-skinned. Flies in for the bay sunset and the new
  white-sand beach; checks into a hotel right on Manila Bay.
- **Maricel "Cel"** — morena Manileña, brown-skinned, street-smart, scraping a living near
  the bay. Job: *writer's pick* (bar staff in Poblacion / sari-sari vendor / call-center
  agent).
- **Dr. Santos** — researcher at UP Manila (health-sciences campus, Ermita, by the bay).
  Developing a compound to fight sarcopenia (age-related muscle wasting in the elderly).
  A spill sends a batch into campus drainage → estuary → Manila Bay → the forbidden zone.

## Mechanism
Skin contact with the contaminated water primes them. Then: every spike of emotion —
fear, thrill, excitement, adrenaline — triggers growth. They chase highs across the city
to grow further. (FMG growth arc — tiers escalate across the story.)
