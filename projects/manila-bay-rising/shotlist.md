## Page-by-page

**P1 — Cold open, UP Manila lab (night).** Establish lab → Santos with the compound → she knocks a tray, a vial shatters, fluid runs to the floor drain → dread beat on the grate. *"The drain goes to the estuary."*

**P2 — The runoff travels (dawn).** Campus canal → outfall pipe into the bay → close on the bawal-lumangoy sign at the cordoned zone. *"And the bay does not care who is warned."*

**P3 — World establish (splash).** Metro Manila at dusk: Makati towers, EDSA taillights, casino glow on the bay. *"Money and heat."*

**P4 — Hae-won arrives.** EDSA taxi crawl → hotel-room bay reveal → walks the dolomite beach → silhouette against the famous sunset.

**P5 — The meet.** Cel (hawking drinks) and Hae-won catch a falling bottle together → they click → "You want to see the real Manila?"

**P6 — Night tour.** Poblacion neon → Entertainment City casino glow from a jeepney → back to the dark bay near midnight. "The water's warm."

**P7 — The line.** They reach the rope cordon + sign. Cel ducks under first. Hae-won follows. *"They ignored the sign."*

**P8 — The dose.** Waist-deep by the outfall plume, the blue-green sheen on their skin. Hae-won feels pins-and-needles; Cel dismisses it.

**P9 — The trigger.** Guard's flashlight → fear/thrill spike → they sprint, and mid-stride clothes pull tight at shoulders and thighs (first visible growth, tier 2) → alley, they see the change on each other.

**P10 — The pattern (button).** Hotel: Hae-won clears the door mark she set that morning → Cel flexes, muscle swells with her pulse → they clock it: *every emotional spike = growth* → low-angle two-shot against the bay + casino glow. *"They went hunting highs."*
