# tmb-daz-study — what the corpus taught, demonstrated on the page

A 3-page remake of *The Mysterious Book* Ch.1's first-transformation beat (corpus source
pages 5-14), rebuilt photoreal-DAZ3D from the lineart, every prompt produced by the
`qa/compose.py` gate chain (no freehand), every page judged by a fresh-context subagent
before banking. Cast bootstrapped from cropped lineart panels: Zara (the grower) + Mia (witness).

## The gate earned its keep
s02 variant 1 was rejected by the fresh-context judge for a *calm* stage-3 face — exactly
the corpus dead-money-shot flaw this page exists to disprove. Swapped to variant 3
(gritted-teeth fierce strain matching s01), which passed. The generator never graded its own
work; the page that would have undercut the thesis was caught mechanically, not by promise.
