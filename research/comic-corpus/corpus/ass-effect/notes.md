# Ass Effect — Analysis (rubric v1.0)

**Source:** https://growgettercomics.com/ass-effect/  **Creators:** Art by Boogie (@boogiedraws) / story uncredited (publisher GrowGetterComics; commissioned by Kray1170)  **Pages:** 23  **Analyzed:** 2026-06-09

A Mass Effect parody: the lead is a Miranda Lawson analogue aboard the "Normandy," with Joker, a krogan "Wrex," and an asari "Samara" as the envy targets. Premise: jealous of bustier/stronger crewmates, Miranda uses her biotics to self-enhance — twice — and the second attempt spirals into a power-mad cosmic-scale muscle apotheosis.

## Camera
- **Distance spread across the book:** good in the front half — p6 spans MS/CU/ECU (spread=4), p7 spans MS→ECU (spread=4). Collapses in the back half: p14 is all-ECU (spread=1) and p16/p18/p19/p20/p21 are single-distance pages. The peak trades rhythm for size.
- **Flat-panel hotspots:** **p3 is the worst** — five flat-level talking-head panels in a row (panels 2–6); only the p1 low-hero ramp entrance breaks the level eyeline. Minor flat insets on p2 p2, p5 (bunk setup p1–3), p7 p4, p12 p2.
- **Angle monotony:** the entire cosmic finale (p17–21) repeats the same low-hero front/three-quarter framing — no high, dutch, aerial, bird, or POV variety to distinguish five climactic pages.
- **Best dynamic compositions to steal:**
  - **p18** — size-comparison: the tiny Normandy ship floated against her thigh makes planetary scale instantly legible (the cleverest single growth device in the book).
  - **p21** — the "I. AM. ABSOLUTE!" splash: figure isolated on a clean white void with a dynamic diagonal stride; negative space + raked axis = the strongest image.
  - **p15 p1** — full-body double-biceps reveal, low-hero, the muscle tier landed in one read.
  - **p8 p1** — towering low-hero hero-shot with crew small in FG (scale-contrast), paired with OTS reaction insets (p2–3).
  - **p13** — foreshortened bicep thrust toward camera with BAAM→BA-BAAM escalation across two panels.

## Expressions
- Faces carry the beats well overall and **never go dead during the money pages**. Peak intensity (5) is sustained: p6 p2 ecstasy (head-back GASP/HAAAH), p13 p1 strain / p2 ecstasy, p15 p2 menacing smug ("go a little further?"), p16 rage+ecstasy, p17–21 escalating rage/ecstasy through the cosmic climax.
- The story exploits faces as turns: p4 p3 and p12 p2 use scheming determination CUs to pivot the plot; p15 p2's devilish grin is the hinge from "enough" to "MORE."
- **Underplay (not dead-face) zones:** the setup dialogue (p3 panels 4–6) sits at intensity 2 — appropriate-ish for banter but flat. No true dead-face defect on any growth panel, which is the key thing the corpus screens for.

## Story & structure
- **Hook:** fast and on-niche — p1 cover and p2 CU put the glutes front-and-center, and the men's CLICK voyeurism (p2 p3) plus Samara's bust-flex (p4) load Miranda's jealousy inside four pages.
- **Pacing:** front half is talky (p3, p9 drag); the engine is a clean mirror — bust-envy (Samara) → transformation 1, then strength-envy (Wrex) → transformation 2. The muscle act (p12–21) is well-paced through p17, then **pads** with three near-identical cosmic splashes (p18–20) before the p21 button.
- **Tease vs payoff:** moderate tease, generous payoff. It does delay — two setups before each transformation, a strain build before each release — but once growth starts it dumps escalation continuously rather than edging. *Relevant contrast for the redhead-houseguest edging project:* this book is the opposite model — it pays off hard and fast and keeps paying off until it runs out of scale, which is satisfying once but leaves no anticipation in reserve (hence the p18–20 redundancy).
- **Cliffhanger / continuity:** none. It ends on the climax splash (p21) with no consequence, comedown, or to-be-continued. The back-cover pin-up (p23) silently resets her to the pre-transformation catsuit body with no in-story explanation — a cover gag, not a narrative beat.
- **Dialogue & SFX:** lettering is functional and pun-forward ("space pants," "By Hera's Milky Way," "I. AM. ABSOLUTE!"). **SFX are the standout craft element** — they escalate legibly (BAAM→BA-BAAM, RIIIPP for the clothing tear, KRAGOOOOOM/KATHOOM/KRZZAP at cosmic scale) and carry the growth sensation through the wordless stretches.
- **Clarity:** sequence is legible panel-to-panel; the two-trigger structure reads cleanly and the escalating SFX/size cues make the runaway-growth direction obvious without exposition.

**Narrative arc:** Miranda envies bustier Samara → secretly biotic-enhances her curves overnight → shows off to the crew → a collision with the krogan Wrex plants strength-envy → she pumps biotics into muscle growth, loses control → escalates to planet- then universe-scale, clothing destroyed, screaming for MORE POWER → ends as an "absolute" macro-muscle goddess (cover pin-up silently resets to baseline).

## Strengths to steal
- **p14 multi-panel-progressive:** three stacked ECUs grow abs→abs→glutes stage-by-stage on the same region with layered VWOOMP/BAAAM/BULGE — the exact device the pipeline wants more of.
- **p18 size-comparison:** floating the tiny Normandy against her body sells cosmic scale in one read; a fixed reference beats any prose scale description.
- **p21 climax splash:** clean white-void isolation + diagonal stride = a reusable money-shot template for "final form."
- **SFX escalation ladder:** BAAM→BA-BAAM (p13), RIIIPP clothing tear (p16), KRAGOOOOOM/KATHOOM/KRZZAP (p17–20) — keep growth legible and visceral when dialogue drops.
- **Motivated-cause structure:** each transformation is triggered by a specific envy (Samara's bust p3–4, Wrex's strength p10–11), not a random catalyst — gives the growth narrative stakes.
- **Sustained peak-expression discipline:** intensity-5 strain/ecstasy/rage faces held across every money page (p13–21) — no dead faces during growth.

## Weaknesses to avoid
- **p3 flat-talking-heads:** five consecutive flat-level panels with a level eyeline; the conversation has no camera movement. The book's flattest stretch — the default-failure mode to avoid.
- **Finale angle monotony (p17–21):** five climactic pages all on the same low-hero front framing; no high/dutch/aerial/POV variety to differentiate them.
- **Repeat-splash padding (p18–20):** three near-identical cosmic full-pagers ("MORE POWER"/"YES YES"/"MORE POWER THAN THE UNIVERSE"); escalation-by-repetition blurs the beats.
- **Back-half distance collapse:** p14 all-ECU and p16/p18–21 single-distance — the wide/medium/tight rhythm of p6–8 is lost exactly when variety would sustain the climax.
- **No comedown / cliffhanger:** ends abruptly on the splash with no consequence; p23 resets her silently — undercuts re-read and series pull-forward.
- **Curve act under-delivers (p5–6):** the first transformation is only two pages with a single result panel, so it under-covers zoom relative to the much longer muscle act — uneven scene weighting.
